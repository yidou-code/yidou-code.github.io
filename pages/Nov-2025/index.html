# Study Summary for November 2025

提示

This article records the learning goals and reflections for November 2025.

# Learning Content

This month, the focus was mainly on learning in the follewing areas:

  1. Front9-end Technology

    • Study and research on vue2 and vue3
    • Completion of a small project (not completed)

  2. CET-6

    • Improvement of vocabulary（about 1000） and listening
    • English reading weekly at least three times (every day 1 hour)

  3. python(Major Course)

    • Learning SVN, a version control system
    • the python basic syntax

  4. Data Structure and Algorithms (Major Course)

    • Fundamentals of algorithms

  5. Discrete Math
